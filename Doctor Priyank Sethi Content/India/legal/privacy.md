## What we collect

- Information you submit via the [E-Consult](/e-consult) form: name, contact details, dental condition description, optional selfie / X-ray.
- Information you submit via the [Contact](/contact) form: name, contact details, message.
- Standard server logs and Google Analytics measurement data (de-identified).

## How we use it

Solely to respond to your enquiry and provide consultation. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing.

## Retention

Enquiry submissions are retained for as long as needed to provide care and meet record-keeping obligations.

## Your rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data by emailing **enquiry@stunningdentistry.com**.
