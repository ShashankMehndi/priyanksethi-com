# About Dr. Priyank Sethi

BDS · MDS · Ph.D. (Faculty of Dental Sciences)

- Our Founder and CEO, Dr. Priyank Sethi established this luxury brand for the class of patients who wish to have No Compromise Dentistry .
- An alumnus of the prestigious Peoples College of Dental Sciences and Research Center, he graduated and completed his post graduation from the same institute in the field of Conservative Dentistry and Micro Endodontics.
- He further received advanced training in Digital Smile Designing and Full Mouth Rehabilitation from Germany.
- Internationally Certified Digital Smile Designer
- He is one of the few in the country who have been awarded with Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) in Dental Sciences.
- He is recognised as a troubleshooter when it comes to treating failed & complex dental cases successfully.
- He has till date successfully treated more than 1000+ patients from around the globe including patients from The United States of America, The United Kingdom, Australia, New Zealand, South Africa, United Arab Emirates, Europe and holds a global fame and reputation amongst dental practitioners worldwide.
- He is a preferred dentist by the families of Ministry of India Officials.
- He is extremely famous amongst his patients for his personal touch, integrity and comprehensive treatment planning.
- He has been an avid clinician and has several national and international research publications on his name. He is one of the most loved celebrity dentist of the country and he believes that every patient is special & unique and so should be the way he or she is treated.
