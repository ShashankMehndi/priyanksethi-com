# No-Compromise Dentistry

A short statement of the principles that guide every case at Stunning Dentistry. v1 scaffold — Dr. Sethi's voice goes here in 300–500 words.

## What it means in practice

- **Diagnosis before treatment plan.** Every case starts with a full clinical assessment.
- **Materials chosen on merit.** No swap-outs to hit a price point.
- **Treatment planned for life.** Cosmetic and structural goals balanced against decade-out outcomes.
- **One operator, one outcome.** The principal who plans the case sees it through.
